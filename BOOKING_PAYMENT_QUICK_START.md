# Booking Payment System - Quick Start Guide

## For Nail Techs: Setup Your Wallet

### Step 1: Navigate to Settings
1. Open the app
2. Go to **Tech Settings**
3. Scroll to **Payouts** section

### Step 2: Setup Stripe Connect Wallet
1. Click **"Setup Wallet"** button
2. You'll be redirected to Stripe
3. Complete the onboarding form:
   - Business information
   - Personal identification
   - Bank account details
4. Submit for verification

### Step 3: Verification
- Stripe will verify your information (usually instant)
- You'll receive email confirmation
- Return to app to see **"Active"** status

### Step 4: Start Receiving Payments
- Once active, you can receive booking payments
- Funds automatically transferred to your bank account
- View earnings in Stripe Dashboard

## For Clients: Book and Pay

### On iOS App (Native)

1. **Browse Techs** → Find a nail tech
2. **Select Service** → Choose service and time
3. **Choose Design** → Select or upload design
4. **Book Appointment** → Review details
5. **Pay with Apple** → Complete Apple In-App Purchase
6. **Confirmation** → Receive booking confirmation

### On Web Browser

1. **Browse Techs** → Find a nail tech
2. **Select Service** → Choose service and time
3. **Choose Design** → Select or upload design
4. **Book Appointment** → Review details
5. **Pay with Stripe** → Enter card details
6. **Confirmation** → Receive booking confirmation

## FAQs

### For Techs

**Q: When do I receive payment?**
A: Payments are transferred to your bank account according to Stripe's payout schedule (typically 2-7 business days).

**Q: Can I see my earnings?**
A: Yes! Click "View Dashboard" in the wallet section to access your Stripe Express Dashboard.

**Q: What if I don't setup a wallet?**
A: You won't be able to receive payments from bookings. Clients can still book, but payment will be held until you setup your wallet.

**Q: Are there any fees?**
A: The platform takes a 12.5% service fee. Stripe may charge standard processing fees (typically 2.9% + $0.30).

**Q: Can I change my bank account?**
A: Yes, access your Stripe Dashboard to update banking information.

### For Clients

**Q: Is my payment secure?**
A: Yes! Payments are processed through Apple (iOS app) or Stripe (web), both industry-leading secure payment processors.

**Q: When is my card charged?**
A: Your card is charged immediately when you complete the booking.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policies are set by individual nail techs. Contact your tech directly for refund requests.

**Q: Why different payment methods?**
A: iOS app uses Apple In-App Purchase (required by Apple). Web version uses Stripe for flexibility.

**Q: Do I need an account?**
A: Yes, you need to create an account to book appointments.

## Troubleshooting

### Tech Wallet Issues

**Problem:** "Setup Wallet" button not working
- **Solution:** Check internet connection, try again, or contact support

**Problem:** Verification taking too long
- **Solution:** Check email for Stripe verification requests, may need additional documents

**Problem:** Payouts not arriving
- **Solution:** Verify bank account details in Stripe Dashboard, check payout schedule

### Client Payment Issues

**Problem:** Payment failed on iOS
- **Solution:** Check Apple ID payment method, ensure sufficient funds

**Problem:** Payment failed on web
- **Solution:** Check card details, ensure sufficient funds, try different card

**Problem:** Booking not confirmed after payment
- **Solution:** Check email for confirmation, contact support with booking ID
